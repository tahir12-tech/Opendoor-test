Running your estate

The management view of the portal: analytics across your whole estate, the fee and commission, settlements, exports, and managing your agencies, branches and users.

What you can see

Where a referrer sees only their own referrals, management sees the whole estate — every agency, branch and referrer in your partner. You can add and manage your own users, and every tracking and analytics surface aggregates across your network. You can't edit canonical records that opndoor owns, or portal settings.

Estate analytics

The dashboard's Sent → Paid → Deed Issued funnel, volume charts and breakdowns aggregate across your estate, reframed by the time-period selector (from the last 7 days through to all time). Period-clipped breakdown tables are labelled within the selected period, so a partial-period figure is never mistaken for a lifetime total.

The fee & commission

The guarantor fee is one month's rent per referral. Commission is split from that fee:

PartyShare of the fee
Partner25%
Agent (the referring agency)10%
Referrers never see commission
  • Fee and commission figures show to management and opndoor admins only.
  • Referrer-tier performance exports have every partner and agent commission line and column stripped out.

Settlements

Commission settles on a monthly cycle: each settlement covers the prior calendar month, is calculated net of refunds, and is payable on the 15th of the following month. The settlement view gives one figure per partner with the constituent applications behind it.

Exports

  • Performance export — the analytics for the selected period as a branded workbook (referrer-tier exports omit commission).
  • Applications — the underlying records for the current view.

Managing agencies & branches

On the Agencies & branches screen you can edit an agency or branch's details, and the change takes effect instantly. Click any figure to drill through to the applications behind it.

Managing users

From User management you can invite users, set their role (management or referrer), and from each user's menu:

  • Reset password — sends a reset link.
  • Reset 2FA — clears their authenticator so they re-enrol at next sign-in (audited). You can do this for your own partner's non-admin users; opndoor handles opndoor staff.
  • Deactivate / reactivate — controls whether they can sign in.

The League

The full League ranks every agency, branch and referrer, sortable by any metric and paged through in full. Rows drill through to the matching Applications filter — a referrer row opens that referrer's applications, an agency row opens the agency's. What each referrer sees on their own leaderboard is controlled from Manage partner (the single lever); changing it prompts a confirmation, because it changes what every referrer at that partner sees.

Filtering applications

The Applications screen adds two filters for your tier:

  • Referrer — narrow to one referrer's applications, populated from your scope's referrers.
  • Period — the dashboard's time-period options, bucketed on sent date, with the status chips recounting within the selected period.

These compose with the existing status, agency, branch and search filters, and honour deep-links (?agency=, ?branch=, ?referrer=) from the League and Agencies screens.
