Running the platform

The internal opndoor view: reconciliation, partners and rates, the underwriter bordereau, system health, the opndoor team, and the HubSpot sync.

Reconciliation

Agencies and branches created on the fly by referrers land in Reconciliation for review. For each, confirm it as a new canonical record, or (in a later release) merge a likely duplicate.

  • Head-office folding — a single-office agency's automatic "[Agency], Head office" branch folds into the agency card; confirming the agency confirms both. The queue counts decisions, not raw rows.
  • Sync nudge — confirming a record fires an immediate HubSpot sync (fire-and-forget) so it appears within seconds; the couple-of-minutes cron remains the backstop.

Partners & rate management

Partners carry a partner commission rate and an agent commission rate. You manage these on the partner record.

The rate-snapshot law

Every commission, settlement, league and export figure reads the rates snapshotted onto the application at creation — never the partner's current rate. Editing a partner's rate changes future referrals only; it never moves history. This is deliberate and load-bearing across the money surfaces.

The premium bordereau

The bordereau export (opndoor superadmin only) is the underwriter's monthly schedule, produced verbatim to the underwriter template: grouped two-row headers (Reference / Tenant Details / Property Details / Guarantee Details), the fixed 18-column order, Landlord Name, DOB always populated, the premium rate in the header block, and the premium amount per policy (monthly rent × rate), with the template's status vocabulary.

Health

The Health screen is the operational status view for the platform's integrations and background jobs — the place to confirm the money and sync paths are running before you rely on them.

The opndoor team

The opndoor team screen manages internal opndoor staff accounts, separate from partner users. Actions taken by opndoor staff appear as "opndoor" on partner-facing screens, never as a referrer.

HubSpot sync

Confirmed agencies and branches sync one-way to HubSpot.

AspectBehaviour
CadenceAutomatic every couple of minutes (cron), plus an immediate nudge when you confirm a record.
ManualThe Sync HubSpot button on Reconciliation runs it on demand.
What syncsApplicants, companies (agencies/branches) and their associations; portal-minted companies carry their commission rate across.
What never syncsHubSpot-owned finance, owner and attribution fields — a defensive filter drops them even if they ever appear in a payload. The sync never writes Contacts; tenants never become Contacts.
